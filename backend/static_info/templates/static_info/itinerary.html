The Day

We hope that you'll join us at the Vancouver Rowing Club in Stanley Park on Saturday August 29th, 2015.

The afternoon will begin with a patio ceremony overlooking the seawall and harbour toward the afternoon sun. We ask that all guests please be seated by 3 p.m.

Our guests will then be invited to enjoy a cocktail at the Rowing Club and tour the park while we take a break with our wedding party for some nostalgic photography.

Once our photographer is satisfied with us, we'll return to the Rowing Club for an evening of dinner, drinks and dancing.
