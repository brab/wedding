Travelling to Vancouver

The weekend of our wedding is High Season in Vancouver so everything is going to be busy; book things early to make sure you don't miss out!

Downtown Vancouver is full of fantastic places to eat, and amazing things to do. VancityBuzz has some great recommendations for nearly everything you might like to do in our beautify city!

If you'd like to book a hotel, we'll post some information here on a few hotels near The Vancouver Rowing Club soon.

Flights

There are many flights into The Vancouver Airport on a daily basis (sometimes even hourly). From the airport there's a rapid-transit line that will take you directly to Downtown in only twenty-five minutes.

Accommodation

We're working on reserving a selection of hotel room blocks for our out-of-town guests and we'll post information here as soon as it's available.

Getting There

We encourage all of our guests to take public transit or a taxi to and from our celebration.

For those that would like to drive, parking in Stanley Park, near the Vancouver Rowing Club, is plentiful if you don't mind a short walk. All parking will cost $11.00 per day.

Weather

Typically, on August 29th, the sun is out, the afternoon warms up to a comfortable 20°C and the evening cools to around 14°C. It's unlikely that we'll see any rain, but it is Vancouver, so you never really know for sure.

Environment Canada is usually pretty accurate with their forecasts. They don't do long term predictions, but if you take a look before you come our way you'll have a pretty good idea how it'll be when you get here.
